Services
Business Services

Equity Partners provides a full service range of business services to businesses and high net wealth individuals. Our focus is helping people achieve their financial goals and mapping performance against pre-determined goals.


We provide the main stream accounting services for all entities including company, trust, superannuation fund, partnership and joint venture. The services we provide include:


  • Financial Reports preparation - annual and interim, where required
  • Income Tax Returns - for all types of entities
  • Fringe Benefit Tax Returns
  • Activity Statements - BAS & IAS
  • Corporate registry and company secretarial services

The team at Equity Partners have worked locally within Canberra for many years and as such have an extensive network of professional relationships to allow us to assist in all aspects of our clients business. Our clients are in many and varied industries. We specialise in providing services to:


  • Building and Construction industry
  • Engineers
  • Architects
  • Retailers
  • Medical Practitioners
  • Consultants
  • Financial Planners
  • Legal Firms
  • Fitness industry

We work with you to keep your business on the right track and operating under best practice. We can work with you to identify and support the pursuit of new business opportunities for growth within your business. Our proactive planning and strategic direction ensures that advantages can be taken within statutory requirements to achieve desired outcomes. To this end we can assist you in providing the following services


  • Restructuring to facilitate tax outcomes as well as being mindful of succession planning and estate issues
  • Asset protection and wealth accumulation
  • Sourcing of finance including liaising with financial institutions and legal representatives to achieve desired outcomes
  • Tax planning
  • Estate planning
  • Succession planning
  • Review of shareholder agreements and other contracts to ensure it is suitable for your circumstance

Our qualified team have extensive experience with all types of accounting software and when required, are able to assist with any data entry questions. We also strongly believe our best work can be done when the information we receive is in order. To this end we are able to recommend assistance from a pool of qualified bookkeepers who will deal directly with you to collate your information into a data file. Once this is done we will prepare your financial statements and income tax returns and discuss with you how to help your business grow.
