Justin Barry - Director
(BComm (Acc), CA, Registered Tax Agent)

Justin Barry is a founding director of Equity Partners. Justin is a highly regarded accountant within the Canberra and surrounding region, with experience in both private sector and Government environments. Justin commenced his career in 1992 in a small Cooma based accounting firm and then joined a large national firm. Deciding that he wanted to have a more personal relationship with his clients, Justin joined a local second tier accounting firm and was made an Executive Director. Justin is a Chartered Accountant and Registered Tax Agent.

With significant experience in a business services environment, primarily dealing with high net wealth individuals and medium sized businesses, Justin can assist you in achieving your business and financial goals. His experience has been with all stages of the business life cycle, and includes tax structuring, business planning, board attendance and dispute resolution. With contacts in the Canberra market place, he can also assist people in contacts and leads to assist you in your business.

Justin is very passionate about achieving the right outcomes for his clients. He is very hands on with all aspects of clients business and his primary objective is to attain the best result for our clients. Justin's commitment and dedication to his clients has gained him a respect amongst his peers within the Canberra community across all professions. This has resulted in a very loyal client base that includes some of Canberra's long standing and well known businesses in many varied industries.
